docManager is an app that enable you to create, store and share documents with friends an family. It provides a platform to share thoughts, plan, idea and any other information with people and also gets feedback that can be very beneficial on your writeups.

Share Documents

Create simple documents on topics that are of utmost concern to you you can also edit and update documents
Create Documents

Saved documents can be viewed by all users, hence you are sharing your thoughts with everyone. They can also give you their opinion on ideas, and issues
View Everyone's Documents

You have access to tons of documents created by others, its an easy way to learn, discover and gain inspiration
